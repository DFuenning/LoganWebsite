The Logan Story

Founded in 1958, Logan Machine has been generating quality machines and products for a wide variety of industries for generations. We consistently provide our customers with the products they need to succeed. From simple stampings to complex tire building machines, Logan is the source.

Housed in a climate-controlled 96,000 square-foot facility, our various departments and operations, using state-of-the-art equipment, provide Logan customers with a broad range of options that solve problems, improve quality, ensure on-time delivery and control costs. We are proud of the highly trained, experienced people who work at Logan. They are our most important asset.
